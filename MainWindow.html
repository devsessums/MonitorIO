Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I use the services offered by this application?
A: All you have to do is sign up or login, and get verified by the system as a user who is allowed to use the services offered by this application!

Q: How do I view the vitals of a patient?
A: To view the vitals of a certain patient, you need to be verified by the system as:

  1. A medical professional designated to that certain patient
  2. A social worker designated to that certain patient
  3. That very certain patient
Then click the "Patients" link on the navigation bar and search for that certain patient. Enter the authorization code for the admitted patient and you will arrive immediately at the vitals for that certain patient.

Q: How do I view the files of a patient as a medical professional?
A: To view files of a patient, you need to be verified by the system as a medical professional. Once you are verified, you can view a patient's file if you are that patient's designated physician.

Q: How do I view the files of a patient as a social worker?
A: To view files of a patient, you need to be verified by the system as a social worker. Once you are verified, you can view a patient's file if you are that patient's designated social worker.

Q: How do I view the files of a patient as an admitted patient?
A: To view files of a patient, you need to be verified by the system as an admitted patient. Once you are verified, You can view your own files that we have on record.

Q: How do I update patient information?
A: To update the files of a patient, you need to be verified by the system as a medical professional. Once you are verified, you can open your patient's file, navigate to what you want to change. Then simply click the "Edit" button to modify information. The system will save the information and update it after your session.

Q: Is it possible to update patient information as a social worker or patient?
A: Updating patient information on the website as a social worker or patient requires authorization. This can be done through lab results or the submission of a few documents in-person or online via the system.
